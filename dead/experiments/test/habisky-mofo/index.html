Forbidden field guide entry no. 1000

Skunkass Habisky Mofo

A legendary marsh plant said to bloom once every thousand years, releasing a stink so ancient it makes raccoons apologize to society.

Officially Unofficial Description

The Thousand-Year Skunkass Habisky Mofo grows where abandoned lawn chairs sink into mud and forgotten gods lose their keys. Its waxy purple-green leaves curl like accusing fingers, and its bloom glows faintly under moonlight, mostly to warn anything nearby that biology has made another terrible decision.

Habitat Swamps, cursed gardens, behind sheds, and anywhere dignity goes to decompose.
Aroma Wet skunk, old batteries, swamp prophecy, and suspicious barbecue smoke.
Warning Do not water after midnight unless you enjoy paperwork from the spirit realm.
