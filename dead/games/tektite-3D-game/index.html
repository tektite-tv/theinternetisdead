Welcome to the rolling wilderness. You are a physics ball exploring a procedurally generated chunky little world, because apparently spheres need purpose too.

Goal: roam around, learn the terrain, flatten trees by touching them, then click flattened trees to collect them as bushes.

Movement: use WASD or Arrow Keys to roll. Hold Shift on land to sprint. Press Space to jump. Press Shift + Space for a stronger launch jump.

Camera: drag with right/middle mouse, use Q/E to rotate, and scroll to zoom.

Water: when underwater, Space floats upward and Shift sinks downward. Yes, the ball has submarine ambitions.

Inventory: press I or click the Inventory tab to slide the 7-slot inventory open/closed. Hold Left Shift while clicking a bush or rubble stack to pick it up, drop, merge, swap, or right-click-drag to spread one item per slot. Double-click any stack without Shift to gather matching items up to a full stack. Rubble in the red right slot can be fired with left click.

Rubble Vacuum: hold Left Control near loose rubble to absorb it into the player.

Menu: use this dropdown to switch between Tutorial, Controls, Customization, and Stats for Nerds.
